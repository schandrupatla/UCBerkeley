Sridevi Chandrupatla

I have been developing software applications throughout my career. While doing so, I developed an interest to understand UX experience and design. I am now on a new journey to explore this passion. I enjoy solving user-centric UI design challenges and problem-solving. My strengths lie in my strong communication skills, observational skills, thirst for learning, and being a team player. I am a good listener and believe analyzing the problem from the user’s perspective helps to empathize with the user. I believe it’s important to know people at a deeper level and understand the “why” behind the decisions in order to effectively understand their needs. There are always multiple ways to solve a problem but giving the most feasible solution is most important. For my projects I used tools like Figma, Adobe Xd, Invision, Miro, and Trello.
